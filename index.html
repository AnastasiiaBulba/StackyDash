How to Play

Stacky Dash is a relaxing arcade game where you collect tiles, build bridges, and dash to the finish! Swipe in any direction to move, plan your route, and enjoy the satisfying gameplay. Ready to become a tile master?

Controls

  • Swipe left/right/up/down to move
  • Collect as many tiles as possible
  • Build bridges to cross gaps

Tips & Tricks

  • Plan your path to maximize tile collection
  • Avoid dead ends and use bridges wisely
  • The more tiles you stack, the higher your score!

Bridge Construction

Build bridges by collecting tiles to cross gaps and reach new platforms. The longer the bridge, the further you can go — but be careful! Each tile counts, so plan your route wisely to avoid running out before reaching the next safe spot.

How It Works:

  • Collect tiles scattered along the path to increase your bridge length.
  • Use the tiles strategically to build bridges over gaps between platforms.
  • Longer bridges help you access hidden or bonus areas with more rewards.

Tips for Success:

  • Prioritize routes with more tiles to keep your bridge supply full.
  • Avoid unnecessary detours that waste tiles and risk falling.
  • Watch out for tricky gaps that require precise timing to cross.
  • Use bridges to create shortcuts, but always have enough tiles to complete them.

Challenge Yourself:

  • Try building the longest possible bridge without stopping.
  • Combine bridge-building with tile collection to maximize your score.
  • Experiment with different strategies to find the optimal path.
