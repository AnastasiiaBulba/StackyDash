Bridge Mastery
Top tips and tricks for building the perfect bridge every time.
Building bridges is the heart of Stacky Dash. After dozens of hours playing and experimenting, I’ve uncovered some essential tips for new and experienced players alike:

  • Always collect extra tiles before attempting long bridges.
  • Look ahead to spot the safest landing spots and avoid dead ends.
  • Use short bridges to access bonus tiles that can extend your run.
  • Practice timing to place tiles precisely and avoid falling.
Master these techniques, and you’ll be unstoppable on your way to the top of the leaderboard!
